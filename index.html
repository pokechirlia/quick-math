What is this about?

I made this web app to practice my web development skill. I also want to play this game to improve my calculation skill :)

How to play?

Just press PLAY, then try to solve as many equations as possible within 1 minute. Type in your answer in the box just above the PLAY button, then press NEXT to move on to the next question. The game will check your answer and add to your score if correct. This web will save your best score for each play.
